ABOUT US

Hydraverse is the result of a collaboration between two siblings from northern Argentina, a unique team that brings together worlds that rarely meet. Their profiles, detailed below, combine scientific rigor with digital creativity:


ROSARIO MEDINA (Team Founder): She is a Biology Teacher and Front-End Developer. Her academic background includes studies in Information Systems Engineering and a Bachelor's degree in Rural Administration, also having extensive training in Conservation and Environmental Education. She has served as the team leader in both editions of the NASA Space Apps Challenge. Her preparation and experience allow her to tackle complex problems with an analytical and structured approach, ensuring that our solutions are both innovative and practical.

LIONEL SOTO (Team Co-Founder): As a high school student, he brings creativity, digital art, and a fresh perspective to our challenges. His passion for science and problem-solving ensures that our solutions are not only robust but also engaging and user-friendly.

Together, they combine academic knowledge and youthful creativity to tackle complex challenges, creating purposeful solutions with a personal touch in every line of code.
